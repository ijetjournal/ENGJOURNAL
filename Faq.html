IJRET - Frequently Asked Questions:

    Q . How to submit my paper?

    Ans : You have to submit your paper through online submission link submit your paper.


    Q .How much it takes for review process?

    Ans : The International Journal of Engineering & Science takes 3 - 5 days for publication of paper.


    Q .When I have to deposit fees?

    Ans : You have to deposit fees only after getting acceptance letter.


    Q .What are publication charges?

    Ans : Publication charges are 1400 INR or 61(USD).


    Q .How to pay fees?

    Ans : 1.You can pay through Online using credit/Debit card/ netbanking /cash card/ etc.
    2.By directly depositing in IJIRAE bank account holder through nearby bank branch.
    3.By transferring fees through their net banking option.
    4.For more clarification feel free to mail us at IJRETchief@gmail.com.


    Q .Do I need to submit the copyright form along with the manuscript?

    Ans : No, copyright forms shall be accepted by IJRET only if the submission is accepted for publication..


    Q .Where can I find Copyrights Transfer Form?

    Ans : Kindly refer to Author Instructions link. If your paper is selected, download the Copyright Form from the author instruction page and send it with the final version of paper.
